Starboy - The Weeknd

(feat. Daft Punk)

About the Song

"Released as the lead single from The Weeknd's third studio album in 2016, 'Starboy' is a captivating fusion of R&B, pop, and electronic elements. Featuring the iconic collaboration with French electronic duo Daft Punk, the song immediately captivated audiences with its infectious beat and memorable hooks. 'Starboy' explores themes of fame, success, and the darker aspects of celebrity culture, offering listeners a glimpse into the high-stakes world of stardom."

About the Artist

"The Weeknd, born Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, has cemented his status as one of the most influential figures in contemporary music. Emerging from the Toronto underground scene, The Weeknd's unique blend of R&B, pop, and electronic influences quickly garnered widespread recognition. Known for his haunting falsetto vocals and introspective lyrics, The Weeknd has amassed numerous awards and accolades throughout his career, solidifying his place as a true musical innovator."
